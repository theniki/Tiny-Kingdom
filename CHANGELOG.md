- 2026-04-19 — [CODE] — **Selection UX refined (D-010 update).** Single-click on any friendly unit or building now *replaces* the current selection — standard RTS behavior. Previous "preserve unit selection on building click" was a defensive hack that forced users to right-click-then-click to switch from units to building. New approach: `onPointerUp` captures `selectedUnits` into `_savedSelection` **before** `_handleClick` runs; if a second click on the same target completes a double-click within 350ms, `_handleActionCommand(target, snapshot)` executes with the snapshot — so double-click-on-farm still sends villagers to work even though the farm is selected after the first click. `_savedSelection` is cleared on right-click, ESC, drag commit, and successful double-click.
